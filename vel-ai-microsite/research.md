# Research brief — Vel AI (vel-ai.com.au)

## Sources & caveats

The live site is unreachable from this build environment (network policy blocks the
domain), so this brief is assembled from search-engine snapshots of vel-ai.com.au and
its page metadata. All visual design here is original — the film recreates the brand's
offer and voice, not their current layout. Anything not confirmed is marked.

## The brand

- **Vel AI Consulting**, Australia. Page title: "Automate Your Business & Save Time |
  Contact Vel AI Consulting" — contact is the primary CTA.
- Offer: business automation for non-technical owners.
  - **AI receptionist (Voice AI)** — trained per business, sounds natural ("most
    callers don't realize they're talking to AI"), handles interruptions, answers
    service questions, transfers to a human when needed, full call transcripts.
  - **Workflow automation** — pre-built templates: lead follow-up, appointment
    reminders, client onboarding. "Most customers fully operational within 24 hours."
  - **Data unification** — replace multiple tools, one view of the business.
- Positioning: "built for business owners, not engineers"; onboarding wizard,
  setup under 20 minutes, no coding.
- Tiers: solo entrepreneurs → growing businesses → agencies & enterprises.
  (Specific prices not visible in snapshots — do not invent them.)
- Support: responds in under 2 hours.
- Proof on their site: testimonials reporting **20+ hours/week saved** and tool
  consolidation. Use only as "customers report…" — no named clients visible.

## The real customer

Australian small-business owner — tradies, clinics, studios, small agencies. They ARE
the business: on the tools, in the meeting, on the other line. Admin happens at night.

- **Pain (their words):** "I can't get to the phone." "I do quotes at 10pm."
  "Follow-ups fall through the cracks." "I don't have time to learn software."
- **Fear:** a missed call is a customer calling the next name on Google.
- **Already tried:** voicemail (nobody leaves one), a VA (expensive, off-hours gaps),
  DIY tools like Zapier (abandoned — "not an engineer").
- **Desire:** the phone handled, the follow-up sent, the evening back.

## Differentiation angle

Every AI-agency competitor site is the same: dark hero, gradient blob, "Harness the
power of AI", feature grid. The film must NOT talk about AI as technology. It tells
one story: a phone call you missed, then the same call answered. Vel is the mechanism;
the owner is the hero.
